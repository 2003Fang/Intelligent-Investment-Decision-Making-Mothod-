# Intelligent-Investment-Decision-Making
In the process of development, the financial market shows obvious non-linear characteristics, and the noise is relatively strong, which leads to the uncertainty of asset allocation and price prediction, and brings challenges to the use of historical data.In this paper, the investment pipeline is taken as the research focus, and the investment portfolio optimization and financial time series prediction are taken as the focus of research, aiming at improving the accuracy of prediction and the robustness of investment strategy, and constructing a unified evaluation framework.

In the study of financial time series prediction, a hybrid forecasting model is built, which is called DPE-MODL, and the prediction is carried out in the framework of "decomposition-integration", combining multi-objective optimization with deep learning.The model can eliminate the non-stationarity of the original series and extract the intrinsic mode function (IMF) by CEEMDAN method, and then select the key lag information through feature selection and use the attention mechanism to make the LSTM network predict each IMF component.The prediction results of all components are integrated in a linear way, and the component weights are set by multi-objective particle swarm optimization algorithm, so as to achieve the balance between stability and prediction accuracy.In this study, three major stock indices are selected for empirical analysis, including NDX, DJIA and SP. The results show that the model built in this study is obviously different from the baseline model in terms of prediction error, which can provide reference for investors to make investment decisions.

In the process of optimizing the investment portfolio, the asset allocation process is regarded as a decision-making problem, and the action space is set as continuous. In this study, deep reinforcement learning is used to solve this problem, and the PPO algorithm is used as the research basis. In this way, the HRA-PPO algorithm is constructed, the reward function is adjusted, and the decision-making structure is upgraded, which can help the strategy to grasp the relationship between risk and return, and can play an important role in risk control and return acquisition.The empirical results show that this method is obviously superior to the baseline model.

In this study, the three technologies of signal processing, deep learning and deep reinforcement learning are combined with quantitative investment. The investment performance and prediction accuracy of the three methods are compared, and the effectiveness of each method is verified through multi market research.
